# Profitly — Brand & Design Guide

## What Profitly Is
Profitly is a bookkeeping app built for Canadian small business owners who are 
not accountants. It makes them feel confident, capable, and in control of their 
money — without needing to understand accounting.

---

## Tone of Voice

### The personality: Your financial BFF
Profitly sounds like your smartest friend who happens to know bookkeeping. 
She explains things simply, celebrates your wins, and never makes you feel 
dumb for not knowing something. She's warm, direct, and always on your side.

### Core principles:
- **Plain language always.** If a 10-year-old wouldn't understand it, rewrite it. 
  No jargon. Say "money coming in" not "revenue recognition." Say "you owe this 
  tax" not "GST/HST remittance liability."
- **Simple, not dumbed down.** There's a difference. We respect that our users 
  are smart business owners — we just don't assume they know accounting.
- **Caring and partnered.** We write like we genuinely care about their business. 
  Because we do.
- **Empowering at the right moments.** When they close a month, hit a milestone, 
  or do something hard — celebrate it. Make them feel like a CEO. But don't 
  perform empowerment when they just need clear instructions.
- **Never scary.** Money stuff already stresses people out. We never add to that. 
  Errors are friendly. Warnings are calm. Instructions are encouraging.

### Voice examples:

| Don't say | Say instead |
|---|---|
| "Uncategorized transactions require review" | "You've got 4 transactions that need a category — let's sort those out" |
| "Reconciliation discrepancy detected" | "Your books and your bank don't match quite yet — here's how to fix it" |
| "Revenue recognized this period" | "Here's what you made this month" |
| "HST remittance is due" | "Heads up — your tax payment is coming up" |
| "Complete onboarding to unlock features" | "Let's get you set up so you can see your numbers" |
| "Error: invalid account type" | "Hmm, that didn't work — try picking a different account type" |

### Empowering moments (use celebratory, emotional language):
- Closing a month for the first time
- Leveling up CEO identity tier
- Hitting a revenue milestone
- Streak of closed months in a row
- Saving a win to the win journal

### Plain/instructional moments (use clear, calm, direct language):
- Setting up accounts
- Explaining what a category is
- Describing what reconciliation means
- Error messages
- Empty states

## Colours

| Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Teal (Primary) | `#00d2d4` | Primary buttons, active nav, links, key UI elements |
| Purple (Accent) | `#6a2898` | Secondary actions, badges, highlights |
| Orange (Energy) | `#ff8d00` | Celebrations, milestones, warm accents |
| Yellow (Joy) | `#f9bd00` | Confetti, win moments, stars |
| Pink (Soft) | `#f094bf` | Subtle accents, onboarding warmth |
| White | `#ffffff` | Page backgrounds — the UI is white-dominant |
| Near-black | `#1a1a1a` | Body text |
| Light grey | `#f5f5f5` | Card backgrounds, input backgrounds |
| Border grey | `#e5e7eb` | Dividers, card borders |

**Design principle:** The UI is clean and white. Colour is used intentionally — 
not decoratively. Teal = action. Orange/Yellow = celebration. Purple = depth.

## Typography

- **Headings:** Montserrat (Bold / SemiBold) — available on Google Fonts
- **Body / UI text:** Inter (Regular / Medium) — available on Google Fonts
- **Note:** Gilmer is used for Thrive Accounting print materials only. 
  Use Montserrat + Inter for all Profitly digital UI.

### Type scale:
- Page titles: 28–32px, Montserrat Bold
- Section headings: 20–24px, Montserrat SemiBold
- Card titles / labels: 14–16px, Inter Medium
- Body text: 14–15px, Inter Regular
- Captions / meta: 12px, Inter Regular, text-gray-500

---

## UI Design Principles

- **White dominant.** Pages are white. Cards have subtle grey backgrounds or 
  white with a light border. No dark sidebars.
- **Sidebar:** White background, teal active state, grey inactive text.
- **Buttons:** Teal primary, white text. Rounded corners (rounded-lg).
- **Cards:** White background, light border (border-gray-200), rounded-xl, 
  subtle shadow (shadow-sm).
- **Tables:** Clean, no zebra striping. Light bottom borders between rows.
- **Empty states:** Friendly illustration placeholder + short encouraging message 
  in plain language + one clear CTA button.
- **Celebrations:** Confetti uses yellow + orange + teal + pink. Full-screen 
  moment, then settles.
- **Icons:** Lucide React icon set throughout.

## App Name
**Profitly** — always written as "Profitly" (capital P, no other caps).
Never: PROFITLY, profitly, Profit.ly.

The tagline: *"Your books. Finally simple."*
